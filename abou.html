Leading the steel structure industry with innovation, quality, and expertise since 2019. Building India's infrastructure with precision and reliability.

Who We Are

Star Vertex Infrastructures is a certified steel structure fabrication and erection company based in Ranchi, operating since 2019. We specialize in providing comprehensive steel structure solutions for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects across India.

Our team consists of skilled professionals who understand the intricacies of steel structure fabrication and erection, ensuring that every project meets the highest standards of safety and quality. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in the construction industry.

We believe in building strong relationships with our clients through transparent communication, reliable delivery, and exceptional workmanship. Our focus on client satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success and growth.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by Mr. Nishant Kumar, Star Vertex Infrastructures began with a vision to revolutionize the steel structure industry in India. With a background in mechanical engineering and extensive experience in the field, Mr. Kumar identified the need for reliable, high-quality steel structure solutions in the growing Indian infrastructure market.

Starting with a small team and limited resources, we quickly gained recognition for our commitment to quality and timely project delivery. Our early success on projects like AIIMS Bilaspur established our reputation in the industry and paved the way for larger, more complex projects.

Today, we are proud partners of Jindal Steel and Power and have successfully executed numerous high-profile projects across the country, contributing to India's infrastructure development while maintaining our core values of quality, safety, and client satisfaction.
